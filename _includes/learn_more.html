My Story

I commited myself to learning how to code while completing my master's degree. I quickly realized that working in HR wasn't what I wanted to do with my time on Earth. All my life I have been creative. Drawing, writing, music, dancing, acting--I've always enjoyed expressing myself. However, my dream of being a rock star wasn't going so well. Still, I knew that in order to be happy I needed to pursue a line of work that allowed me to be creative and challenged me at the same time.

Teaching myself to code has been one of the most challenging and fun experiences of my life. Working on a coding project gives me the same sort of rush that I get from writing a new song. There's still a lot that I need to learn, but that's part of the fun of coding. I'll never stop being a student, and I'll never stop pushing myself to be better.

I record indie pop under the name Bridge Underwater. I'm currently working on a new album that I hope to have out in 2018.
